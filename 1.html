About us

Global Capacity Development Center(GCDC) was estabilished in 2018, to explicity focus on the capacity development of staff in Good Neighbors Partnership.

GCDC is committed to building the competencies of GN Staff all around the world by offering a diverse range of training opportunities from hands-on, practical training on technical skills needed for filed implementation of community development programs to supporting organizational development.
